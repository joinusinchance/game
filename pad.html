How to Play

Cover the calendar grid with the 8 puzzle pieces so that only today's date remains exposed:

  • The Month (e.g. MAY)
  • The Day of Month (e.g. 28)
  • The Day of Week (e.g. THU)

Controls:

👉 Tap a piece in the tray below to select it. Tap again to deselect.

🔄 Use Rotate/Flip H/Flip V to orient your active piece.

🎯 Tap any cell on the board to place it.

🧹 Tap a placed board piece to select it, then tap Remove to return it to the tray.

How to Play

Cover the calendar grid with the 10 puzzle pieces so that only today's date remains exposed:

  • The Month (e.g. MAY)
  • The Day (e.g. 28)
  • The Day of Week (e.g. THU)

Controls:

👉 Tap a piece in the tray below to select it. Tap it again to deselect.

🔄 Use Rotate / Flip H / Flip V to orient your selected piece.

🎯 Tap any cell on the board to place it anchored from its top-leftmost block.

🧹 Tap a placed piece on the board to select it, then tap Deselect to clear active selection.

Stuck? Let our backtracking search solver compute a valid mathematical layout for your current date target.

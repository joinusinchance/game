How to Play

The calendar grid shows the 12 months, 31 days, and 7 weekdays.

Your objective is to place all 8 polyomino puzzle pieces onto the board so that they cover exactly 43 cells, leaving **only 3 cells uncovered**:

  • The current Month (e.g., MAY)
  • The current Day of Month (e.g., 28)
  • The current Day of Week (e.g., THU)

Controls:

👉 Tap a piece in the bottom tray to select it.

🔄 Use the Rotate/Flip buttons to orient your selected piece.

🎯 Tap any cell on the board to place/move the selected piece to that location.

🧹 Double tap a piece on the board or use the Remove button to put it back in the tray.

Majorcan Highs Rules

Objective

The sole objective is to survive! Players start with 3 lives. The player who loses a life in the final round of a hand is one step closer to elimination. The last player standing wins the Majorcan Highs championship.

Gameplay (The Hand)

  • Each player is dealt **3 cards**.
  • The hand consists of **3 rounds (tricks)**.
  • The player who wins a trick leads the next.
  • The aim of a trick is to play the **highest ranked card**. Suit doesn't matter.
  • Card Ranks (Ace Low, King High): A (Lowest) → 2 → 3 → ... → Q → K (Highest).

The Death (Losing a Life)

  • The final trick is the only one that matters for lives.
  • The player who plays the **single lowest card** in the final trick loses a life.
  • If two or more players tie for the lowest card, no one loses a life, but the highest card still wins the trick.
  • If a player reaches 0 lives, they are eliminated from the game.
